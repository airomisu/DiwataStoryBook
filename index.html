The Legend of The Diwata: Maria Makiling

High above the sun-drenched plains of Laguna rises a mountain whose silhouette tells a story before a single word is spoken. To the wandering traveler, it is a majestic peak of emerald and mist; but to those who know the old ways, it is the profile of a woman in eternal slumber. This is Mount Makiling, the sacred domain of a diwata whose beauty is said to be as timeless as the stones themselves.
